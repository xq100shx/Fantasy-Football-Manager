About This Site

This site is designed to help users manage their fantasy football squads. Users can create squads, assign players, and track their performance based on real-life match statistics.

Player Points Multipliers

Points for players in your squad are calculated based on their performance in matches. The multipliers for different player positions are as follows:

  • Goalkeepers (GK): Points are based on saves percentage.
  • Defenders (DF): Points are calculated using the following multipliers:
    • Goals: 6 points
    • Assists: 4 points
    • Shots on target: 1 point
    • Tackles: 2 points
    • Passes completed: 1 point
    • Successful dribbles: 1 point
    • Yellow cards: -1 point
    • Red cards: -3 points
  • Midfielders (MF): Points are calculated using the following multipliers:
    • Goals: 5 points
    • Assists: 4 points
    • Shots on target: 1.5 points
    • Tackles: 1.5 points
    • Passes completed: 2 points
    • Successful dribbles: 2 points
    • Yellow cards: -1 point
    • Red cards: -3 points
  • Forwards (FW): Points are calculated using the following multipliers:
    • Goals: 7 points
    • Assists: 5 points
    • Shots on target: 2 points
    • Tackles: 1 point
    • Passes completed: 0.5 points
    • Successful dribbles: 2 points
    • Yellow cards: -1 point
    • Red cards: -3 points
